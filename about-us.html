Our Story

MLA Format Generator was founded in 2020 by a group of educators and academic professionals who recognized the challenges students face with proper citation formatting. What started as a simple tool to help students create MLA headers has grown into a comprehensive academic resource center serving over 500,000 students worldwide.

"Our mission is to empower students with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed academically, while simplifying the complex world of citation formatting."

Today, we're proud to be a trusted resource for students, educators, and researchers across the globe. Our platform continues to evolve, adding new features and resources to meet the changing needs of the academic community.

Our Mission

At MLA Format Generator, we believe that proper formatting shouldn't be a barrier to academic success. Our mission is threefold:

  • Simplify Citation: Create intuitive tools that make citation formatting accessible to everyone
  • Educate: Provide comprehensive resources to help students master academic writing standards
  • Empower: Give students more time to focus on research and content by automating formatting tasks

Why MLA Formatting Matters

Proper MLA formatting is more than just a requirement - it's a fundamental academic skill that:

  • Demonstrates attention to detail and academic integrity
  • Gives proper credit to original authors and researchers
  • Helps readers locate your sources easily
  • Creates a professional presentation of your work
